Dear Fairshots community, It is with a heavy heart that we announce the end of operations for Fairshots.org. This decision was not made lightly, and we understand that this may come as a disappointment to many of you.

We would like to extend our gratitude to our dedicated users and supporters who have helped make Fairshots a reality. Your contributions and passion for making a better world through photography have been a source of inspiration for us.

Together, we collaborated on many meaningful projects that helped make the world a better place through the power of photography. Your talent and creativity have left a lasting impact, and we are proud to have been a part of your journeys.

Despite this setback, we remain optimistic about the future and are excited to pursue new endeavors that will continue to support and promote both the photography and NGO's communities.

Thank you for being a part of the Fairshots community. We hope to stay in touch and continue to support each other in our future endeavors.

To see a record of what we were for a while, you can find us on Instagram: @Fairshots

Sincerely,
The Fairshots Team
